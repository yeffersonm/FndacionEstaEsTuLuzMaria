SOCIAL objective



The foundation "THIS IS YOUR LIGHT OF HOPE" is an organization of common utility, non-profit, at the service of the community open to all people. In the development of this fundamental purpose, the foundation may direct, plan, execute, coordinate, evaluate, organize and perform all kinds of activities of interest in society, mainly in management and purposes related to social service, competitiveness, recreation , sports, prevention in health, protection of children and the elderly, the environment, agricultural activities, technological development, employment generation, training for peace. For the fulfillment of the corporate purpose, it will develop the following actions:



Manage resources with national and international governmental and non-governmental entities in order to develop social programs that benefit communities.

Carry out programs and days of sensibility in vulnerable communities that help welfare processes and improve their quality of life.

Carry out cultural projects that favor the integral development of the communities.

Conduct psychosocial care days that help strengthen the communities.

Promote the creation of companies in the communities of influence to facilitate their welfare and their social and economic development.

Promote sport as a healthy lifestyle in vulnerable communities

Promote health campaigns of all kinds through agreements

Formulate and execute business development and participatory projects
